Niyam — Play Store screenshots

Six marketing frames in the brand palette (sunrise + bottle-green), built from real app screens. Rendered at 1080×1920 (phone & 7″ tablet) and 1440×2560 (10″ tablet) — both valid 9:16 within Play's size limits.

How to upload in Play Console → Store listing → Graphics:
  • Phone screenshots: upload all six from out/phone/
  • 7-inch tablet: upload the same six from out/phone/ (1080×1920 qualifies)
  • 10-inch tablet: upload the six from out/tab10/ (1440×2560)
Files are PNG, well under 8 MB each. Re-run python3 generate.py + the Chrome render step to regenerate after any copy change.
