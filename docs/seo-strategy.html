AEO — being the answer engines' answer

  • FAQPage schema on /faq with 8 canonical Q&As (the exact questions people ask assistants).
  • MobileApplication + Organization schema on the homepage; Article schema on every post.
  • Every page answers its question in the first two sentences (extractable snippets).
  • After launch: Play Store listing + site must agree word-for-word on key facts (price, languages, claims) — consistency is an AI-citation signal.
  • Wikipedia-style neutrality in the category article keeps it citable.

Technical checklist

  • Static, no JS framework, <1s loads; semantic HTML; canonical, OG/Twitter cards, sitemap.xml, robots.txt, favicon set.
  • cleanUrls (no .html); all pages mobile-first.
  • Google Search Console — needs Pranav (5 min, his Google account; verification file is my side).
  • Bing Webmaster (free, feeds Copilot answers) — same pattern.
  • After Play launch: appId links / app indexing, store badge with UTM.

Cadence

Batch 2 (4 devotional articles) within days; afterwards ~2 articles/week alternating devotional × problem clusters, each batch interlinked and added to the sitemap. Blog deliberately unlinked from the hero flow — it serves search, not visitors, exactly as Pranav specified (it IS linked in the footer, which engines need for crawl discovery).
