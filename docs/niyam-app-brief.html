Niyam — Product Brief

नियम · An India-first Android app · www.myniyam.com · June 2026

One line: Niyam places a mantra between you and your distracting apps — a 15-second sacred pause before every scroll.

The problem

Most of us open Instagram, YouTube or Facebook dozens of times a day without deciding to. App blockers exist, but they treat the user like a suspect — walls, timers, shame. They get uninstalled within a week because nobody wants to live with a punisher.

The insight

Niyam is not a wall; it's a threshold. The moment you open a distracting app, Niyam appears first with a mantra you chose — Gayatri, Mahamrityunjaya, a Gita verse — in your script, with its meaning, and a 15-second countdown ring. You read. You breathe. Then Continue, and the app opens; Niyam stays out of the way for the next five minutes. The scroll is never forbidden — it's consecrated. Discipline through devotion, not denial. That is the name: niyam (नियम) — observance, the yogic practice of self-discipline.

The practice loop

Each mantra read counts toward a 14-day sadhana — a journey with one mantra. Daily streaks build, the journey completes with a small celebration, and the next sadhana begins. A library of 26 authentic mantras — Vedic, Upanishadic, Bhagavad Gita, and devotional classics — each with source citation, transliteration and meaning.

Truly Indian, by construction

Everything works in 9 display languages — scripts, meanings, and the entire interface, down to the unlock screen's Continue button.

Privacy as principle

No accounts, no sign-up, no personal data. Detection and progress live entirely on the device. The accessibility service reads nothing but app launches.

Business model

Freemium. Blocking and the mantra pause are free forever, with 5 starter mantras and two languages. Premium — ₹15/week · ₹49/month · ₹399/year, with a 7-day full trial — unlocks all 26 mantras, all languages, unlimited switching, and removes the small banner ads. Three commitments: the overlay never carries an ad, is never locked, and an active journey is never broken by a paywall.

Look and feel

Warm, modern, alive — sunrise gradients, floating cards, the Outfit typeface, pumpkin orange (#FF6400) on bottle green and eggshell. It looks like dawn, not like detention.

Status — June 2026

Feature-complete in sandbox: blocking engine, 26-mantra library, journeys and streaks, full visual identity with dark mode, 8-language interface, paywall with trial timeline and day-6 reminder — 123 automated tests, every sub-project independently code-reviewed.
